Cartier International SNC or simpl Cartier , is a french luxury-goods conglomerate that design, manufactures, distibutes, and sells jewelry, leather goods, watches, sunglasses and eyeglasses. Founded by (Lous-Francois) Cartier 1819-1904 in Paris in 1847, the company remained under family control until 1964. The company is Headquartered in Paris and is a wholly owned subsidiary of the Swiss ( Richemont Group). Cartier operates more han 200 stores in 125 countries, with three Temples ( Historical Maissons) in paris, London< and New York City.
